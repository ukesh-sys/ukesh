RNA Modification and its Impact

RNA modification is common phenomenon, which have both negative and positive impact. For instance, some RNA modification can promote stability of mRNA overall, which could have a positive impact on its translation to protein and it can also go other way around. All kind of modification is carried out by three kinds of proteins, which can be classified as writers, erasers, and readers, depending upon their roles.

RNA modifications present its aftermath on wide variety of biological mechanisms, so understanding of its fundamental is crucial to determine prognostic and diagnostic role. There are variety of modification that happens in RNA; among them the most common RNA modification is m6A, which usually occurs in the beginning of the 3’ UTR near stop codon. Its presence has been detected on wide array of genes and extensive study has been going on to understand it role.
